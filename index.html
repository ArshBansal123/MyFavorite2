My Favourite Hobby
Soccer

About

Soccer is my favourite sport. It is the most fun sport ever. We run around kicking the ball in goals. I want to be a proffesional soccer player when I grow up. My idol is Lionel Messi. Here is a short poem on soccer-
I like soccer,
Such a fun game.

Want to score,
And practice more.

In Soccer, we kick, run and throw,
Passing the ball to and fro.

Kick the ball,
To score a goal.

Win the game,
And claim your fame.

Soccer is my passion,
No one can stop my fascination!
